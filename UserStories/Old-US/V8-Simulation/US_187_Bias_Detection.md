# US_187: Bias Detection (ML Agent)

## Mission
Automated identification of overconfidence and systematic errors in model predictions across different segments (leagues, odds ranges, time of season).

## Mission Details
1. **Bias Segmentation**: Analyze performance by:
    - Favorites vs Underdogs.
    - Specifically high/low market volatility.
    - Home vs Away bias.
2. **Alerting**: Identify leagues where the model is systematically "overconfident" (e.g., predicted 80% win but actual 60%).

## Success Criteria
- [ ] Bias report integrated into the validation dashboard.
- [ ] Discovery of systematic model flaws.
